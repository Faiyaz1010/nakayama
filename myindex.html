Nakayama Hydro Power Site works & Visit
Site 1: Works: Assembly, installation and commissioning of MHPP

Works

    The Asaji Micro hydropower plant in Oita Prefecture is made of two containers: a 6ft container where we assembled all the mechanical parts of a Kaplan turbine (propeller turbine with adjustable inclination of runner blades) and a 12 ft container of all the electrical parts. One of the problem encountered is that the guide vane was not opening fully, so we connected it directly to the actuator. From this first-hand experience, I also learned how to monitor turbine data like water flow and speed using the dashboard tracking of parameters such as water flow, turbine speed, and power output.
Site 2: Runner and Bearing replacement

Works

    At the Taguchi MHPP (Kirishima City, Kagoshima Prefecture), we replaced a damaged runner and learned how to check motor alignment, water flow and pressure inside the pipe
Site 3: Annual maintenance

Works

    At the Shiraito no taki site there was water leakage and a burnt control panel. I carried out full inspection and cleaning, as there was no output initially. After completing the work, the output was restored, and I prepared a detailed report of the issue and the actions taken.
Site 4:Installation and commissioning

Works

    The Toryusu micro hydro power plant is not a containerized type. Turbine and generator installation were handled on site using crane. I learned to perform Pulley-belt setup and alignment, follow up testing procedures with and without water.
